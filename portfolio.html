Works

This is a typeface poster. The typeface is Frutiger. This typeface is used mostly for direction signs. Therefore, I added some arrows to emphasize the use of the font. For the color, I chose some bright colors such as yellow because I want to catch attention just like a sign needs to be eye-catching.

This poster is created with photoshop. The goal of this poster is to create a poster of a leader in the arts or sciences who is no longer living. I chose to do Kate Spade because her style of design was very bright and joyful. I designed the poster with some elements of her in the background with some bright color scheme to represent her style of color.

For this poster, I used both Photoshop and Illustrator. Our task for this project is to design an event poster. The poster is designed for a night market with different food and games. Therefore, I used some darker colors to emphasize the night atmosphere.

This project is a vexel art project using Illustrator. I used mainly the pen tool and pencil tool for this project. I chose to use the black and white color scheme to show the contrast between black and white.

This is the first scientific drawing I have ever drawn. I used micro pens to draw this piece. Overall, I found doing scientific drawing pretty interesting. This piece also took me quite of a bit as I was trying to scale the portion of the skeleton correctly.

Other than drawing, I have also tried some photography. I really like this photo because it shows the action the football player with a very concentrated facial expression. This facial expression shows her determination of getting the flag even though this might not be a good professional photo.
